# Privacy Policy for TubeNotes

> This program will not transfer any information to other networked systems unless specifically requested by the user or the person installing or operating it.

TubeNotes is a local-first application. It does not collect, store, or transmit any user data to external servers.

**Last Updated:** February 2026

TubeNotes is designed with a **privacy-first** and **local-first** philosophy. We believe that your notes and thoughts should belong to you and stay on your machine.

---

## 1. Data Collection and Transmission
* **No Personal Data:** TubeNotes does not collect any personally identifiable information (PII).
* **No Tracking/Telemetry:** We do not use any analytics, tracking pixels, or telemetry services to monitor your usage.
* **No External Servers:** Your notes, kanban boards, and canvas data are never transmitted to external servers or third-party cloud providers.

## 2. Data Storage
* **Local Storage:** All your data is stored locally on your device using a SQLite database (managed by Prisma).
* **File Access:** The application only accesses the directories necessary for its operation and the files you explicitly interact with.

## 3. Internet Usage
TubeNotes only uses your internet connection in the following specific scenarios:
* **Update Checks:** The app may check GitHub Releases to notify you if a new version is available.
* **External Links:** If you click on an external link within your notes, it will open in your default system browser.
